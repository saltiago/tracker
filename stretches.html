stretches
hip flexors & anterior chain
Thomas test position
Lie on edge of table or bed. Pull one knee to chest, let other leg hang. Hold 60–90s per side. Focus on releasing, not pulling.
Kneeling rectus femoris stretch
Low lunge, back knee down. Posteriorly tilt pelvis (tuck tailbone) before reaching back foot up. Hold 60s per side.
90/90 hip flexor
Front leg at 90°, back leg at 90°. Shift weight forward over front hip. Keep torso tall. 60s per side.
posterior chain
Standing hamstring
Foot elevated at hip height. Hinge forward at hip, spine neutral. Avoid rounding lower back. 45–60s per side.
Figure-4 glute stretch
Supine. Cross ankle over opposite knee, flex foot. Pull thigh toward chest. 60s per side.
thoracic & shoulder
Thoracic extension over foam roller
Roller perpendicular to spine at mid-back. Arms crossed or behind head. Extend gently over roller. 5–8 reps per segment, move up incrementally.
Doorway pec stretch
Forearm on door frame at 90°. Step through until mild tension. Hold 45s. Vary arm height for different pec fibers.
Lat stretch (overhead)
Hold doorframe or pole overhead with one hand. Step back and shift hips opposite direction. 45s per side.
adductors
Copenhagen (passive)
Side-lying. Top leg elevated on bench at hip height. Let bottom leg hang toward floor. Hold 60s per side. Progress to active holds.
Butterfly / groin
Seated, soles together, elbows pressing knees down. Hinge forward at hips, spine tall. Hold 60–90s.
